How To Recommend a Taxi Stand Location

  1. Close this box.
  2. Find the location of your suggested taxi stand on the map, and then drag the map until the crosshair is over the spot you wish to select (NOTE: The "+" and "-" signs in the upper left can be used to zoom in and out).
  3. After the crosshairs are over the spot you want, click on the green box in the lower left corner to submit your choice.
  4. You can also agree with others by selecting locations already recommended.
